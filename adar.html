The Programme

Join Strategic Insights and Analytics for a practical six-week programme designed to build confidence in data preparation, statistical analysis, visualisation, interpretation, and evidence-based decision-making. Participants will work with real-world datasets while developing applied capability in R, Power BI, and Microsoft Excel.

Learning Outcomes

What You Will Gain

  • Solid understanding of data concepts and study designs.
  • Expertise in data cleaning, exploration, and visualisation.
  • Proficiency in R statistical software, Power BI, and Excel.
  • Competence in descriptive and inferential statistics.
  • Hands-on capacity to interpret and apply analytical results to real-world problems.
  • Certificate of Completion issued by SI Analytics.
  • Access to training materials, datasets, and post-training support resources.
  • Networking opportunities with researchers, analysts, clinicians, and programme staff across Nigeria.
Who It Is For

Who Should Attend?

This programme is open across sectors. It suits anyone building applied analytical and statistical capability, including:

  • Data Managers
  • Data Analysts
  • Public Health Researchers
  • Clinicians
  • Monitoring and Evaluation Personnel
  • Postgraduate Students (MPH, MSc, PhD)
  • Government Ministry Staff
  • NGO Staff
  • Professionals seeking practical data-analysis training
  • Individuals seeking applied analytical and statistical capability

Prerequisites: Basic computer literacy is required. No prior R programming experience is necessary. Participants should have access to a functional laptop and reliable internet.

Curriculum

Course Curriculum Overview

A focused, applied curriculum that moves from foundational statistics to practical, dataset-driven analysis and reporting.

  1. 1Descriptive and Inferential Statistics. Core statistical concepts underpinning sound analysis.
  2. 2Data Cleaning and Preparation. Structuring and validating datasets for reliable analysis.
  3. 3Data Visualisation Using Power BI. Building clear, decision-ready dashboards.
  4. 4Interpretation and Application of Statistical Outputs. Turning results into evidence.
  5. 5Practical Exercises with Real-World Datasets. Applied, hands-on analysis throughout.

Throughout the programme, learners will gain exposure to:

  • R
  • Power BI
  • Microsoft Excel
  • Data preparation
  • Statistical interpretation
  • Analytical reporting
